Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

Apostille Path is designed to minimize data collection. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Document contents or uploads
  • Email addresses (unless you contact us)
  • Physical addresses

What We Do Collect

When you use our wizard, we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (page views, clicks)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Aggregated routing selections (for improving accuracy)

This information is used solely to improve the service and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Payment Information

When you purchase a detailed checklist, payment processing is handled by a secure third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card information.

Cookies

We may use minimal cookies for:

  • Remembering your wizard progress (stored locally in your browser)
  • Analytics (anonymous, aggregated data)

No Document Handling

We never handle, process, or store your documents. The service provides routing information only. You submit documents directly to government offices.

Third-Party Links

Our service includes links to official government websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we collect. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data, there is typically no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about data we may have collected, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date will reflect when changes were made.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the repository on GitHub.
